U.S. Workforce Exposure to CS3D High-Risk Sectors

The European Union's Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CS3D) threatens American jobs. State-level employment in the most at-risk sectors: manufacturing, energy and mining, and agriculture.
Sources: BLS QCEW (2024 Annual Averages)

Methodology: State-level employment data is derived from the Bureau of Labor Statistics' Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages (QCEW) 2024 annual averages. To ensure an apples-to-apples baseline of corporate regulatory exposure, the analysis measures UI-covered wage-and-salary workers in NAICS 31-33 (Manufacturing), NAICS 21 (Mining, Quarrying, and Oil and Gas Extraction), and NAICS 11 (Agriculture, Forestry, Fishing and Hunting) as a percentage of total covered employment. Self-employed proprietors and family farmers are excluded.
