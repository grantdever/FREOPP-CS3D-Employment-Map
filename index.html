U.S. Workforce Exposure to CS3D High-Risk Sectors

The European Union's Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive (CS3D) threatens American jobs. State-level employment in the most at-risk sectors: manufacturing, energy and mining, and agriculture.
Sources: BLS QCEW (2024 Annual Averages)
Color map by:

Methodology: State-level employment data is derived from the Bureau of Labor Statistics' Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages (QCEW) 2024 annual averages. The analysis measures UI-covered wage-and-salary workers in NAICS 31-33 (Manufacturing), NAICS 21 (Mining, Quarrying, and Oil and Gas Extraction), and NAICS 11 (Agriculture, Forestry, Fishing and Hunting) as a percentage of total covered employment. Self-employed proprietors and family farmers are excluded. State totals may not perfectly reflect all jobs due to standard BLS data privacy suppressions in certain sectors. Suppressed data points are marked accordingly.
